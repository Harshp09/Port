ABOUT ME

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Jabalpur Engineering College, located in Jabalpur, in 2012. Following that, I pursued and successfully completed my Master's degree in Electrical Drives from Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (M.A.N.I.T) in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, in 2016.

Since 2017, I have been serving as an Assistant Professor within the Department of Electrical Engineering at Delhi Technological University, under the Government of N.C.T Delhi. In this role, I have had the privilege of contributing to the academic and intellectual growth of students while furthering my own research endeavors.

Presently, I am deeply engrossed in my Ph.D. journey within the Department of Electrical Engineering at the esteemed Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, situated in New Delhi, India. My doctoral research is centered around the realms of power electronics, electrical machines, and drives. I am committed to advancing knowledge and innovation in these areas, aiming to make meaningful contributions to the field.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have continually sought opportunities to expand my understanding and expertise in electrical engineering, driven by a passion for learning and a desire to make a positive impact in my field.
